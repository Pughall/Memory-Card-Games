
Right-click on your webpage and select Inspect.
Alternatively, you can press F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Option+I (Mac).
Go to the Console Tab:

In the Developer Tools, navigate to the Console tab.
Clear Local Storage:

Type the following command and press Enter:
localStorage.removeItem('leaderboard');
This will clear the leaderboard data stored in the local storage. Refresh the page to see the changes.
